The economics of counterfeit drugs;
Understanding the cost and impacts on public health

Why are counterfeit medicines a major public health concern? In the worst case, some contain dangerous products. According to Interpol, “some fake medicines have been found to contain mercury, arsenic, rat poison, or cement.” Consuming counterfeit drugs can have serious consequences for individuals, communities, and overall global public health.

Consumers education;
How patients can verify the authenticity of their medication.

How do you check for authentication of medicine? The most basic thing you should check is the seal. If you find that the seal is broken or has been tampered with, do not buy it. Make sure you buy medicine in which their seal is intact. Checking the packaging can also give you an idea regarding the authenticity of the medicine.

The importance of drug authentication in combatting counterfeit drugs;

The taskforce recommends that pharmacists (1) purchase medications from known, reliable sources; (2) warn patients of the dangers of purchasing medications over the Internet; (3) confirm with distributors that products were purchased from manufacturers or other reliable sources; (4) monitor counterfeit product alerts; (5) examine products for suspicious appearance.
