About Us

Welcome to MedTrust

We are committed to revolutionizing medication authentication to ensure patient safety and combat the circulation of counterfeit drugs. Our platform empowers patients, healthcare professionals, and regulatory bodies with advanced authentication technologies, proactive verification measures, and transparent processes.

MedTrust Key Features

  • Real-Time Authentication: MedTrust offers instant verification of drug authenticity, providing healthcare professionals, patients, and insurance companies with peace of mind.
  • Notification Alerts: Stay informed with timely alerts about counterfeit or authentic drugs, enabling informed decision-making and proactive actions.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Our intuitive design makes drug authentication simple and accessible for everyone, empowering healthcare stakeholders to make informed choices effortlessly.

How It Works

Step-by-Step Process

1. Enter Drug Details

  • Input the drug's brand name, batch number, or scan the QR code using your smartphone or device camera.
  • Ensure accurate input of information to facilitate seamless authentication.
  • If scanning the QR code, ensure proper lighting and focus for optimal scanning perfromance.

2. Authentication Verification

  • MedTrust's advanced technology instantly verifies the entered drug details against a comprehensive database of authentic medications.
  • You'll receive immediate feedback on the authentication status, indicating whether the drug is verified as authentic or flagged as potentially counterfeit.

3. Receive Notification

  • Upon completion of the authentication process, MedTrust sends immediate notifications regarding the drug's authenticity status.
  • Notifications are delivered in real-time via in-app alerts, ensuring timely awareness of the drug's verification outcome.
